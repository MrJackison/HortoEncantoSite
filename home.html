About


Everyone starts somewhere!

  • 2018

    The beginning

    We started as very small botanical entrepreneurs, we planted vegetables and Sometimes flowers at home, we sold them around the neighborhood and we managed to make a little money with it.

  • 2019

    Consolidation

    After much thought, we decided to go headlong into this business, we saw that flowers were more profitable and we invested in more species and purer genetics, we expanded our sales location, we delivered flowers around the city according to the demand on our networks.

  • 2020

    Establishment

    With a lot of effort and enthusiasm, we managed to rent an establishment close to the center of the city, where we would do our flower shop, the place needed some maintenance and repairs here and there, but it was ours, that's what mattered.

  • 2021-2023

    Now

    Now we are a reference in our city when it comes to flowers, we sell several species, depending on market demand, we also have species exchanges seasonal.
